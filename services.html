Detailed Services

What We Offer

Each service is designed with empathy, clinical precision, and a deep commitment to survivor dignity and long-term healing.

Service 01

Psychosexual Medicine & Sexual Wellness

Specialized care addressing sexual dissatisfaction, trauma, and emotional well-being, particularly among women. This service integrates both physical and psychological aspects of sexual health, breaking stigma and promoting awareness.

  • Addressing intimacy issues and sexual dissatisfaction
  • Healing trauma and shame related to sexuality
  • Promoting comprehensive sexual education for women
Service 02

Trauma-Informed Care & Survivor Support

A survivor-centered approach designed to support individuals affected by trauma, particularly gender-based violence. The focus is on restoring dignity, emotional safety, and long-term healing.

  • Safe, confidential, and empathetic care sessions
  • Listening circles and guided healing processes
  • Long-term trauma recovery frameworks
Service 03

Women's Mental Health & GBV Support

Focused mental health services tailored to women, addressing emotional, psychological, and societal challenges including gender-based violence, stigma, and systemic barriers.

  • Support for survivors of abuse and violence
  • Emotional resilience and recovery programs
  • Breaking stigma around women's mental health

Treatment Approach

The Healing Journey

Dr. Aninda Sidhana's approach integrates clinical expertise with trauma-informed, survivor-centered care. Every individual's journey is treated with empathy, dignity, and personalized attention to ensure long-term healing and transformation.

01

Initial Consultation

Understanding the individual's concerns, history, and emotional state in a safe and fully confidential environment, building trust from the very first session.

02

Assessment & Diagnosis

A structured and compassionate evaluation to identify psychological, emotional, and behavioral factors affecting well-being and informing the care plan.

03

Personalized Treatment Plan

Tailored therapy combining psychosexual medicine, trauma-informed care, and evidence-based practices specifically suited to individual needs and goals.

04

Therapy & Healing Process

Continuous support through structured therapy sessions focused on emotional healing, resilience building, and restoring a sense of self-worth and agency.

05

Ongoing Support & Growth

Long-term guidance, relapse prevention, and empowerment strategies to ensure sustainable mental well-being beyond the immediate therapeutic period.

06

Holistic & Integrated Care

Addressing both mental and sexual health together, ensuring complete, stigma-free, and inclusive healing that honors the whole person.
