About Dr. Aninda Sidhana

A transformative leader in mental health, gender justice, and survivor-led healing systems in India.

Professional Biography

Dr. Aninda Sidhana is a distinguished psychiatrist, psychosexual medicine specialist, educator, and survivor-advocate whose work bridges clinical excellence with deep social impact. With over a decade of experience, she has redefined how mental health and gender justice intersect.

Born into a family of psychiatrists, she completed her MBBS from Padmashree Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, followed by an MD in Psychiatry from Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth, and a fellowship in psychosexual medicine at JSS Medical College, Mysore.

Her career spans clinical psychiatry, academic leadership, and advocacy at IHBAS Delhi, Santosh University, and Dr. S.S. Tantia M.C.H. & Research Centre, consistently focusing on women's mental health, trauma recovery, and breaking taboos around sexual wellness.
