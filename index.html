About Dr. Aninda Sidhana

A transformative leader in mental health, gender justice, and survivor-led healing systems in India.

Professional Biography

Dr. Aninda Sidhana is a distinguished psychiatrist, psychosexual medicine specialist, educator, and survivor-advocate whose work bridges clinical excellence with deep social impact. With over a decade of experience, she has redefined how mental health and gender justice intersect, creating survivor-centered systems of care rooted in dignity, empathy, and innovation.

Born into a family of psychiatrists, she developed an early understanding of psychological well-being and care systems. She completed her MBBS from Padmashree Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, followed by an MD in Psychiatry from Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth, Puducherry, and further specialized in psychosexual medicine through a fellowship at JSS Medical College, Mysore.

Her career spans clinical psychiatry, academic leadership, and advocacy, with key roles in institutions such as IHBAS Delhi, Santosh University, and Dr. S.S. Tantia M.C.H. & Research Centre. Through her work, she has consistently focused on women's mental health, trauma recovery, and breaking societal taboos around sexual wellness.

The Dignity Dialogues

A pioneering survivor-led initiative founded in 2025, reimagining mental health and gender justice through storytelling, listening circles, and trauma-integrated care systems. It has directly supported 500+ survivors and trained 200+ professionals.

Aawaz Initiative

A safe haven for survivors of gender-based violence, designed to provide counseling, support systems, and advocacy platforms that empower individuals to reclaim their voice and identity.

Clinical Expertise

Specializing in psychosexual medicine, trauma care, addictions, and marital therapy, Dr. Sidhana addresses complex emotional and sexual health challenges with an integrated, stigma-free approach.

Academic Leadership

As Assistant Professor and Guest Faculty, she mentors future mental health professionals, integrating trauma-informed care, empathy, and real-world application into education systems.

Advocacy & Media

Through podcasts, media features, and public platforms, she drives conversations on trauma, gender justice, and emotional well-being, reaching over 50,000 individuals globally.

Policy & Social Impact

Contributing to national and global mental health policies, she advocates for survivor-led frameworks, influencing institutional protocols and gender justice systems.

Impact & Vision

Dr. Sidhana’s work has impacted thousands of lives through direct clinical care, survivor support programs, and large-scale digital outreach. Her initiatives emphasize measurable outcomes such as improved well-being, reduced trauma distress, and strengthened community support systems.

Her approach combines evidence-based clinical practice with lived experience, making her work both deeply empathetic and structurally transformative. She believes that healing is not just individual, but collective—requiring systemic change, policy reform, and inclusive dialogue.

With a vision to build sustainable, survivor-centered ecosystems, Dr. Aninda Sidhana continues to lead innovations that redefine mental health care, empower marginalized voices, and shape the future of gender justice in India and beyond.
